About Me

I am the Field CTO at Eficode, where I have spent over a decade guiding organizations through the complexities of modern software development. My focus lies in integrating AI-powered tools, implementing DevSecOps best practices, and advancing Platform Engineering.

From scaling CI/CD in regulated industries to managing GitHub Enterprise Server environments, I help clients turn business goals into scalable, secure technology roadmaps. I am passionate about responsible AI adoption and ensuring that "pets to cattle" infrastructure transitions result in controlled efficiency rather than chaos.

Outside of technology, I enjoy sailing—a hobby that mirrors my professional commitment to precision, strategy, and adaptability.
